Financial & Credit Services

Access the Funding Your Business Needs

Every business requires funding to grow, innovate, and thrive. A strong financial foundation, including a solid personal and business credit history, is essential for securing the capital necessary to achieve your goals. At Wizz Digital, we guide you through every step of the process.

Evaluate Your Financial Situation

Understanding your current financial standing is the first step toward securing funding. Our team works with each client to:

  • Assess personal and business credit history
  • Identify opportunities to improve creditworthiness
  • Provide actionable strategies to strengthen financial profiles

Strategic Guidance on Funding Options

We provide consulting on the types of funding that may suit your business, including:

  • Business loans
  • Credit cards
  • Alternative financing solutions

We also advise on which banks or lenders may align with your needs and help prepare the necessary documentation to support funding applications.

Professional Relationships and Global Support

Our team maintains professional relationships with banks and financial institutions, giving clients informed guidance on potential financing sources. Services are available to both U.S.-based and international businesses and individuals, allowing us to support clients wherever they are located.

Empowering You to Make Informed Decisions

While we provide guidance, strategies, and support, specific funding outcomes, loan approvals, or credit increases cannot be guaranteed. Our goal is to equip you with the knowledge and tools to maximize opportunities and make confident, informed financial decisions.

Who Can Benefit from Our Services

Our financial consulting and business support services are designed for a wide range of clients looking to grow, secure funding, and build strong financial foundations.

Existing Business Owners & Entrepreneurs

Looking to expand, improve credit, or access funding for growth? Our services help you navigate financing options and strengthen your financial profile.

Aspiring Business Owners

U.S.-Based Individuals

Individuals seeking to secure funding or improve their personal and business credit history can benefit from our personalized financial consulting.

International Businesses & Individuals

We provide guidance and consulting for clients abroad who are interested in U.S. funding options, building U.S. credit history, and exploring opportunities in the U.S. market. Outcomes depend on individual circumstances and the approval policies of financial institutions.

Our Process

1

Initial Consultation

Discuss your business goals and financial needs with our experts. This session allows us to understand your current situation, challenges, and objectives. Clients can book this consultation online, and it is paid upfront.

2

Comprehensive Review

Our team analyzes the information you provide, including personal and business financials, credit reports, and other relevant documents. We identify opportunities for improvement and potential funding options.

3

Strategy & Planning

Based on the review, we develop a customized financial strategy tailored to your business. This includes credit improvement suggestions, recommended funding options, and a roadmap for reaching your financial goals.

4

Implementation Guidance

We guide you through implementing the strategy, including preparing documentation, organizing your financial information, and taking steps to improve creditworthiness. Our team provides ongoing support to ensure you are ready to approach lenders or funding sources.

5

Funding Applications

With your strategy in place, we assist in preparing and submitting applications to banks, lenders, or financial institutions. We advise on which lenders may be suitable based on your business and credit profile.

6

Secure Your Funding

Once funding is approved and secured, our process concludes. At this stage, our professional consulting commission is collected. We also provide follow-up guidance to ensure smooth financial management and maintain credit health for future opportunities.
